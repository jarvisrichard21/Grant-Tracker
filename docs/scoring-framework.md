# Grant Readiness Scoring Framework

## Components and Weights
- Business Plan – 25%
- Financial Documentation – 20%
- Community Impact – 15%
- Grant Fit – 20%
- Branding & Presentation – 10%
- Past Experience – 10%

## Scoring Scale
Each component is rated 1–5, where:
1 = Not prepared
5 = Fully prepared

## Formula
Readiness Score = (Σ component_score × weight) ÷ 5
